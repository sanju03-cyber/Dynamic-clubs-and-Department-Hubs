About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering is committed to delivering quality education in computing, software development, and emerging technologies. The department focuses on building strong foundations in programming, problem-solving, and innovative research. With experienced faculty, modern laboratories, and industry-oriented curriculum, the department prepares students for successful global careers.
